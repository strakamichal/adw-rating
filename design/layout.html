About the Rating

Ratings are based on the Plackett-Luce model, updated after each competition. Higher rating equals stronger performance against tough competition. Ratings are comparable across all size categories. Each run is a head-to-head comparison: beat strong teams and your rating goes up more; lose to weaker teams and your rating goes down more.

What affects rating: placement in each run, strength of competitors in that run, and competition tier (Major events count more). What does not affect rating: time or faults directly (only placement matters), breed, or country.

The rating scale is centered around 1500, with higher values indicating better performance. Approximately 68% of teams fall between 1350 and 1650. Teams are classified into tiers: Elite, Champion, Expert, and Competitor.
